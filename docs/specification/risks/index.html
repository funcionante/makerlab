Specifications

Risks

  • Although all the requests of equipment, VMs, VLANs and the like are unmoderated, relying on the users’ good will may lead to a situation where a project has all of the units of a resource in its possession and other projects cannot continue because of this. Moderation can reduce the frequency and intensity of these situations.
  • As usually happens with wikis, bad-intended users may disrupt the common process of the system by, for instance, editing pages of equipments making them unusable. Moderation mitigates the problem.
  • An user not provided with a supported smartphone or a tablet can’t access all the functionality the system. A “community” phone may exist in the room, providing for those who need.
